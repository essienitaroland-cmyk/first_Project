Welcome To Lucious Tee Cake 'n' Treats



We deliver high-quality, Nutritious, and fun lunch packs for your kids,

From fresh pizzas to sushi, sandwiches, wraps and more. We've got something healthy, fresh
and Deliciousfor everyone!

Fun, nutritious meals designed to keep kids energised and healthy,
with all orders made on our easy-to-use app.

The highest quality local produce assembled by accredited food experts, and delivered fresh in refrigerated vans.

Reduce your environmental footprint with our sustainable packaging and unique compost program.

Step 1

ORDERING


Parents simply download our easy-to-use app, create a profile for their child and can then place orders weeks in advance. Depending upon the school, we deliver up to 5 days a week with cut-off times as late as 8am.

Step 2

DELIVERY TIME


We will deliver the lunch packs for the school at least 30 minutes prior to your designated meal time, all in our fleet of refrigerated vans and with each class packed in separate bags to make distribution as easy as possible!

Step 3

MINIMISING WASTE


We will collect the class bags on our next delivery, and we can even recollect any biodegradable packaging for us to compost through our unique recycle and compost program.
